A Series of Me

          Well, hi! My name is Claudine Moneek G. Mejorada, but everyone calls me "Chloe." I’m a third-year college student-athlete at the Technological University of the Philippines-Manila (TUP), pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS).

A little about me—I’m still in the process of discovering who I am (aren’t we all?), but here’s what I do know: I’m an ambivert, often described as someone hard to read. I have a passion for food, reading, and games, and while I may not be the type to love studying, I bring my all to the things that truly matter to me. Badminton is one of those things—it’s my heart and soul. As an athlete representing TUP, I’d leave everything behind to play the sport that’s such an essential part of my life.

Outside of sports, I’m laid-back but tend to avoid extreme thrills like wild rides—they're just not for me! Working out, however, grounds me and helps me refocus. I believe in prioritizing my peace and growth, choosing to invest my time only in things that matter. Above all, I place my faith and family at the center of my life—they’re my foundation and guiding light.
